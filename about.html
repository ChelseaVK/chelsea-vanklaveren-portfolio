Hi! I'm Chelsea Van Klaveren.

Built to create. Coded to connect.

Once upon a classroom, I was an educator fueled by boundless curiosity and a knack for turning questions into adventures. Now, I channel that same inquisitive spark into front-end development—exploring the world of responsive design, semantic HTML, and dynamic interfaces one semicolon at a time. Whether I’m looping through arrays or looping yarn into a cozy scarf, I thrive when I’m making something from scratch. Code, like art, is how I build intuitive interfaces, foster user connection, and bring ideas to life. And when I’m not bringing ideas to life with JavaScript and CSS, you’ll find me sketching, crocheting by the window, or laughing with my family over warm cookies and cool ideas.
